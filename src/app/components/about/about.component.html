Our Story

Cuddlecot is a modern e-commerce platform dedicated to providing the best quality, comfortable, and stylish clothing for children. We believe every child deserves to look and feel amazing while being comfortable in what they wear.

Our mission is to bring comfort, quality, and affordability together in one place, making shopping for kids' clothing a delightful experience for parents everywhere.

Technology Stack

This application is built using modern web technologies including Angular for the frontend, Node.js with Express for the backend API, and MySQL for reliable data storage. The system ensures proper user management, product catalog, cart functionality, and secure order tracking.
